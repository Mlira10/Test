Best Paloma Recipe

What is a Paloma?

A paloma is a Mexican cocktail that is very refreshing. It combines tequila with grapefruit juice/soda and lime.

If you are a fan of margaritas, then chances are you will also enjoy palomas. It is a very popular drink during the summer time.

Ingredients

For this recipe, you will need the following items:
  • Your favorite tequila (I recommend Espolon)
  • Grapefruit juice
  • Grapfruit soda (Squirt or Jarritos)
  • Half of a lime
  • Ice
  • Agave nectar

Putting It All together

  1. Fill a cup with ice
  2. Add one and a half ounces of tequila
  3. Add 3 ounces of grapefruit juice
  4. Add half an ounce of lime
  5. Add half an ounce of agave nectar
  6. Top off with grapefruite soda
