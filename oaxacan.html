Best Oaxacan Old Fashioned

What is a Oaxacan Old Fashioned?

A Oaxacan Old Fashion is a fancy Mexican cocktail drink that infuses reposado tequila and mezcal to create something magical

This unique Mexican cocktail is similar to the classic bourbon based drink.

Ingredients

For this recipe, you will need the following items:
  • Your favorite reposado tequila (I recommend Espolon)
  • Angostura Bitters
  • Agave nectar
  • Mezcal
  • Ice
  • An orange

Putting It All together

  1. Fill a cup with ice
  2. Add one and a half ounces of tequila
  3. Add half an ounce of mezcal
  4. Add 3 dashes of bitters
  5. Add half an ounce of agave nectar
  6. Add an orange peel and stir
